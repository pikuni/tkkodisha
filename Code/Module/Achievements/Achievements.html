Sky is the limit to success. The Cherished dreams of Triveni still awaits realization, in different spheres. During these passing years, Triveni has collected a few diamond pebbles from the glistening shore of culture. A few of these are worth mentioning here.
  1. It has succeeded in grooming a large number of budding talents into acclaimed performers, in the national and international level
  2. It has been able to create a sensation in the arena of traditional dance , through scintillating programmes in various parts of state ,nation , Doordarshan and invited audience programmes of A.I.R.(8 photograph)
  3. It has created a competitive spirit among young dancers, by conducting various competitions in the state level and rewarding the genuine talents (3 photograph)
  4. Triveni has covered some steps in the direction of bringing about cultural integration, through its varying dance items.
  5. It has moved towards amelioration of the suffering of physically and mentally handicapped and bettering the environment. (6 photographs with details)
  6. Triveni Kala Kendra has glorified itself by felicitating a galaxy of noted dancers, singers and musicians, Dr Minati Mishra, Raghu Nath Panigrahi Guru Paschimeswar Jena, Guru Madan Mohan Lenka and many other shining stars (5 photograph)
