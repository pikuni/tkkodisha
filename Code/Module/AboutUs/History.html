The History of Triveni Kala Kendra is the life history of Guru Kishore Kumar Mohanty Taking up cudgels against cultural-perversion, in all levels, and without compromising with opportunistic trends for immediate gains, Guru Sri Mohanty, hailing from Baripada, underwent serious training in Odissi Dance, under the guidance of Padmabibhusan Guru Late Kelucharan Mohapatra. Mr Mohanty, worked as a dance teacher in Kala Vikash Kendra, Cuttack & formed his own dance troupe, Triveni Kala Kendra, for a greater thrust in the cultural horizon. Himself, an amazing performer, Mr. Mohanty has been a role model for his numberless disciples. His life partner Urmila Mohanty has been an ideal Oasis for him in the illusory desert of hopelessness, in the present juncture of cultural deprivation.
The burning fire of creative imagination, the dance patterns and the urge to recreate a trail of success for future generations, moves him and Triveni towards an enlightened horizon.
